Grilled Salmon

Grilled salmon is a healthy and delicious option for dinner.

Ingredients

  • 4 salmon fillets, about 6 ounces each
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 lemon, zested and juiced
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced

Directions

  1. Preheat the grill to high heat.
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, lemon zest and juice, salt, black pepper, oregano, thyme, and minced garlic.
  3. Brush the salmon fillets with the marinade, and let them sit for about 10 minutes.
  4. Place the salmon fillets on the grill, skin-side down, and grill for about 5 minutes.
  5. Flip the salmon fillets, and grill for an additional 3-4 minutes, or until the salmon is cooked to your liking.
  6. Serve the grilled salmon with your favorite sides, and enjoy!
